About

Meet Your Consultant

Expert Care

Dr. Bhoomi Vikani

Lactation Consultant

Welcome to Mother's Touch, a dedicated clinic focused on "Nurturing Mother, Nourishing Babies." Breastfeeding is a natural process, but it doesn't always come naturally. As a certified lactation consultant, my goal is to empower mothers with the knowledge, confidence, and support they need during this beautiful journey.

Every mother and baby dynamic is unique. Whether you are facing challenges with latching, milk supply, pumping, or transitioning back to work, I provide personalized care plans tailored to your specific needs and goals.

Online consultations and Hospital/Home Visits are available for your convenience.
